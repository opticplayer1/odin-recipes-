Crab Rangoon Recipe

Golden curves of perfection, perfectly encapsulating gooey cream cheese and savory alaskan king crab. This recipe will teach you how to make the most moist and crunchy crab rangoon you will ever make. In this recipe you will need these items listen below.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cream cheese softened
  • 4 oz. cooked crab meatalaskan king crab preferred
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion power
  • 24 wonton wrappers
  • 24 oz. vegetable for frying
  • waterfor

Steps

  1. Place the cream cheese,crab,garlic powder, and onion powder into a large bowl. Mix with a hand mixer until everything is well combined.
  2. Scoop 1 heaping of the cream cheese mixture into the corner of one of the corner of one of the wonton wrappers. Dip your fingers in the waters and wet the outer edge of the wonton wrapper. Fold the wonton wrapper over the cream cheese mixture and seal by pinching the edges together. Repeat until the filling is gone and you have about 24 rangoon.
  3. Pour the vegetable oil into a large pot(~5 quarts), making sure there is at least 4-inches of oil. Heat the oil to 375F.Place 3-4 rangoon into the oil and fry for about 1 minute on each side. Remove the rangoon with a slotted spoon and place it on a paper towel.
  4. Serve with your favorites dipping sauce
