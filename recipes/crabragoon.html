Crab Rangoon Recipe

Golden curves of perfection, perfectly encapsulating gooey cream cheese and savory alaskan king crab. This recipe will teach you how to make the most moist and crunchy crab rangoon you will ever make. In this recipe you will need these items listen below.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cream cheese softened
  • 4 oz. cooked crab meatalaskan king crab preferred
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion power
  • 24 wonton wrappers
  • 24 oz. vegetable for frying
  • waterfor sealing
